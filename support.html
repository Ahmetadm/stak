How to play

A block slides back and forth above the tower. Tap to drop it. Line it up with the block below — land it dead-center for a "PERFECT" to keep the block full-size and build your combo multiplier (×2, ×3 and up). Miss the center and the overhang is sliced off, making the next block narrower. Stack as high as you can!

Common questions

How does scoring work? Each block adds to your score; PERFECT drops add a combo bonus that grows the longer your streak.

Is my best score saved? Yes — it's stored on your device.

Why do I see ads? Ads keep Mochi Stack free. A full-screen ad appears briefly between some games.
