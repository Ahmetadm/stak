Privacy Policy

Mochi Stack · Last updated 14 June 2026

Mochi Stack ("the app", "we") is a casual arcade game. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

We do not ask you for any personal information, accounts, or sign-in.

The app shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google's advertising SDK may collect and process:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA / Ad ID)
  • Approximate (coarse) location, derived from your IP address
  • Product interaction and in-app usage data (e.g. ads shown, taps, gameplay interactions)
  • Advertising data (information about the ads you have seen)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)

This data is used for third-party advertising and analytics — delivering and measuring ads and understanding app and ad performance. It may be used to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. On iOS the app asks for your permission (App Tracking Transparency) before using the advertising identifier for personalized ads; if you decline, you still see ads, but non-personalized ones.

Your best score is stored only on your own device and is never sent to us.

We (the developer) do not collect, store, or sell your personal data ourselves, and we do not see your identity.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads — see Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data.

Children

Mochi Stack is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your choices

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and the in-app prompt.
  • You can reset your advertising identifier in your device settings.
